To play this game, follow these rules carefully.

  • To start the game press any key on your keyboard.
  • Follow the same color pattern that computer shows you.
  • Once you have completed the pattern in the next level you will get the next pattern.
  • You have to remember the previous pattern along with the new pattern that you saw.
  • Once you click all the colored tiles in the exact format you will proceed to next level.
  • Even a single wrong click will end the game.
  • To restart the game, press any key to restart.
  • To restart the game in the middle of the ongoing game, refresh the page.
